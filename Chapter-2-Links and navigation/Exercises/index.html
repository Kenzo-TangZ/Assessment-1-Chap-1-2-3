DANDADAN OVERVIEW

"Dandadan" is an anime series that premiered on October 3, 2024, captivating audiences with its unique blend of supernatural elements, romance, and humor. The story follows high school students Momo Ayase and Ken Takakura (nicknamed Okarun) as they navigate a world filled with ghosts, aliens, and psychic powers. Their contrasting beliefs—Momo believes in spirits but not aliens, while Okarun believes in aliens but not spirits—lead them into bizarre and thrilling adventures.
